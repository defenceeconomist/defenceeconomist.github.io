Three companion projects collect source-based notes, teaching material, and applied workflows for evaluators working with quasi-experimental methods and value for money evidence, alongside defence economics teaching and analysis.

Methods and labs

Quasi-Experimental Design Labs

A practical route through quasi-experimental evaluation design, with notes, slides, and runnable labs covering matching, weighting, synthetic control, difference-in-differences, and regression discontinuity.

What you'll find

  • Method notes for matching, weighting, and synthetic control design.
  • Applied labs using teaching datasets and reproducible workflows.
  • Slide decks and presenter scripts for short method introductions.
